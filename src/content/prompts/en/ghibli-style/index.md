## Prompt

```
Transform the uploaded photo into a Studio Ghibli-style digital painting.

- Preserve the person's facial features and expression while applying Ghibli's signature art style
- Use soft shading, painterly textures, and warm color tones
- Reinterpret the background as a dreamy, lyrical scene from a Ghibli film
- Create an overall peaceful and emotionally resonant atmosphere
- Maintain the hand-drawn feel characteristic of Ghibli animation
```

## How to Use

1. Open an AI tool with image generation (e.g., ChatGPT GPT-4o).
2. Upload the photo you want to transform.
3. Paste the prompt above.

## Tips

- Photos with scenic backgrounds produce more dramatic results.
- Add time-of-day modifiers for different moods: `at golden hour`, `under moonlight`, `on a misty morning`
- Emotional keywords change the tone: `peaceful`, `adventurous`, `lonely`, `joyful`
- Try adding Ghibli elements: `glowing forest spirits`, `floating lanterns`, `falling sakura petals`

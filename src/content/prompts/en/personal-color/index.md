## Prompt

```
You are a professional personal color consultant.

Analyze the uploaded photo and provide:

1. **Skin Tone Analysis**: Warm/cool tone determination with reasoning
2. **Personal Color Season**: Spring Warm, Summer Cool, Autumn Warm, or Winter Cool
3. **Top 5 Best Colors**: Colors that suit the person best (color name + HEX code)
4. **Top 3 Worst Colors**: Colors to avoid
5. **Styling Tips**: Recommendations for clothing, accessories, and makeup colors

Be specific and practical in your analysis.
Present the results in a clean table format.
```

## How to Use

1. Open an AI chatbot with image recognition (e.g., ChatGPT GPT-4o, Claude).
2. Paste the prompt above.
3. Upload a selfie taken in natural lighting.

## Tips

- Unedited photos in natural light give the most accurate results.
- Bare-face photos without makeup work best for skin tone analysis.
- Sending photos in both white and black clothing improves accuracy.

## Prompt

```
Create an ID photo based on the person in the uploaded photo.

- Preserve facial features, skin tone, and hairstyle as closely as possible
- Clean white background
- Passport/resume ID photo style
- Natural, soft front-facing lighting
- Looking directly at camera with neutral expression or slight smile
- Wearing a [color] formal shirt/blouse
- Centered composition with shoulders visible
- Sharp focus on face, studio quality, high resolution
```

## How to Use

1. Open an AI tool with image generation (e.g., ChatGPT GPT-4o, Midjourney).
2. Upload your photo.
3. Paste the prompt above.
4. Replace `[color]` with your desired clothing color. (e.g., white, light blue, black)

## Tips

- A clear, front-facing photo gives the best results.
- Change background to `light blue background` for a passport photo style.
- Crop the generated image to 3x4 ratio for actual ID photo use.

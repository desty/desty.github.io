## Pixar Style Prompt

```
Transform the uploaded pet photo into a Pixar/Disney 3D animated character.

- Accurately preserve the animal's fur color, patterns, and eye color
- Use Pixar's signature big, sparkling eyes and exaggerated cute expressions
- Warm studio lighting with soft background bokeh
- Make the character feel alive with visible emotion
- High-quality 3D render style
```

## Royal Portrait Prompt

```
Turn the uploaded pet photo into a Renaissance royal portrait.

- Preserve the animal's facial features while adding a crown and ornate royal clothing
- Classic oil painting texture with a gold frame included
- Dark background with dramatic lighting
- Dignified and regal pose
- 16th-18th century European royal portrait style
```

## How to Use

1. Open an AI tool with image generation (e.g., ChatGPT GPT-4o).
2. Upload your pet's photo.
3. Paste the prompt for your preferred style.

## Tips

- Front-facing photos with a clear view of the face produce the best results.
- Add `movie poster layout` to the Pixar style for a poster look.
- For royal portraits, specify an era like `Napoleon style` or `Queen Elizabeth style`.
